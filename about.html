About Me

I am Binoy Chandra Dey, currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Chemistry at IIT Bhubaneswar under the School of Basic Sciences. My research interests lie at the intersection of quantum dynamics, cavity quantum electrodynamics, and computational materials science.

I completed my M.Sc. in Chemistry (2022–2024) at IIT Bhubaneswar, with a thesis on optoelectronic properties of Cs₂AgBiI₆ double perovskites. I also hold a B.Sc. (Hons.) in Chemistry from Chandernagore College, West Bengal.

I'm passionate about computational research, coding, and scientific writing. I often attend interdisciplinary workshops and love exploring new topics in science and technology.
